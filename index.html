About

I am an undergraduate student in the Paul G. Allen School for Computer Science & Engineering and the Information School at the University of Washington, pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Informatics. I work as a teaching assistant for INFO 330: Databases and Data Modeling, supporting students in SQL and relational database design. I am also a research assistant in the Golden Lab, working on projects applying machine learning and analysis to data from implantable devices.

My academic interests include software engineering, data science, and product development, with a focus on building tools that bridge technical depth with practical impact.
